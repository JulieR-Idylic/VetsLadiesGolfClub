Women’s golf, great community, and a welcoming place to play.

We play Wednesday mornings at Veterans Memorial Golf Course from March through October. No matter your skill, you are welcome to join us for 18 or 9 holes of play.

Events Contact

Who we are

We’re a women’s golf club focused on friendly competition, consistent play, and a supportive, inclusive community.

Welcoming

Golfers of all skill levels are encouraged to participate and improve at their own pace.

Organized

Clear weekly logistics, simple events, and easy-to-find information.

Community-minded

We support local community projects and enjoy giving back when opportunities arise.

Club logistics

When we play

  • Day: Wednesdays
  • Tee times by month:
    • March – April: 10:00 AM
    • May – June: 9:00 AM
    • July – August: 8:00 AM
    • September: 9:00 AM
    • October: 10:00 AM
  • Season: March – October

Cost & joining

  • Dues: $20 per season
  • How to join: Email us for details and next steps

How league play works

  • Weekly games: You can choose to play in our weekly games - a different format each week (e.g., sweeps, scramble, low putts, etc).
  • Season competitions: Optional season-long contests and end-of-season awards.
  • Results: Posted weekly in the Member Area.
