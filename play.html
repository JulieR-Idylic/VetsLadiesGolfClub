League Play

Our league offers weekly game formats you can opt-in to play, plus optional season-long competitions.

Weekly games

Each week features a different game format. This keeps play fun, fair, and interesting for a range of skill levels. You can still play each week without entering into any games.

You must have a GHIN established in order to enter to play in the weekly games.

  • Formats rotate: e.g., low net (sweeps), scramble, low putts, etc.
  • Cost: $2 each week
  • Results: posted weekly in the Member Area.

View results (members)

How scoring works

  • Gross score: the number of strokes you take.
  • Game adjustments: some formats have special scoring rules.
  • Net score: raw score adjusted by handicap and any other adjustment for that week's game.

Note: Specific weekly game rules and payouts are shared with members.

Common weekly formats

Sweeps

Handicap is applied to determine net score. Lowest net wins.

Scramble-Blind Draw

Blind Draw for 18-hole players. All players must play 18 holes. Blind Draw optional for 9-hole players. Players cannot join 18-hole players then drop out at 9.

Low Putts

Count total putts only while on green.

Want more detail? Members can access the playbook and weekly instructions in the Member Area.

Season-long competitions

These run across the season and add a fun layer of progress and recognition.

Eclectic – Birdies

Runs: March – October

Cost: $3

  • Keep track of your birdies on game day.
  • The total birdie pool is divided by the total number of birdies recorded across the field to determine the value of a single birdie, which is then paid out to players based on their birdies.
  • Must be attested by another member

Eclectic – Chip-ins

Runs: March 4 – October 21

Cost: $3

  • Keep track of your chip-ins on game day.
  • The total chip-in pool is divided by the total number of chip-ins recorded across the field to determine the value of a single chip-in, which is then paid out to players based on their chip-ins.
  • Must be attested by another member

Eclectic

Runs: March 4 – October 21

Cost: $3

  • Best score over the course of the season for 18-holes / 9-holes
  • Update your eclectic card each week to indicate the lowest score on each hole over the course of the season

Hole-in-one

Runs: March 4 – October 28

Cost: $5

  • You must be a current club member and have paid into the pot
  • Must be attested by a fellow golfer
  • Does not have to be on a game day but must be at Veterans Memorial Golf Course
  • If we have no winner in a season, the pot is carried over to next season

Club Championship

Takes place: Three designated weeks in August and September

Cost: $15 entry fee

  • Best 2 out of 3 rounds
  • Must play on the designated play dates
  • A, B, and C flights based on GHIN handicap
  • Open to all ladies club members

Most Improved Player

Takes place: April 1 - October 21

Based on: GHIN Reports

Winner announced at end of season lunch
